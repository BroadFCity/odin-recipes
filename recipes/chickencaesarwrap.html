Chicken Caesar Wrap

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, chopped
  • 3 cups Romaine lettuces, chopped
  • 2/3 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup croutons
  • 1/2 caesar salad dressing
  • freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 5 large flour tortillas (or whole wheat tortillas

Steps

  1. Toss all ingredients, except for tortillas, together in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Place a large spoonful of mixture in a line along the edge of a tortilla.
  3. Roll up tightly, like a burrito, and secure with toothpicks, if necessary.
